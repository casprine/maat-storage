## Description

Keep the worker reconciler and orchestrator responsible for scanning the configured document directory, waiting for stable writes, hashing files, registering stable files with the API, handling duplicates/changed paths/deletes, and enqueueing index jobs while TUS remains only an upload path.

## Acceptance

- Partial writes do not enqueue jobs until size and mtime are stable.
- Duplicate checksums, same-path changes, deleted files, unreadable files, and symlink/path safety are tested or intentionally documented.
- Generated artifacts are written under app data and never inside the document directory.
- Worker-to-API registration routes remain protected by worker/admin auth.
