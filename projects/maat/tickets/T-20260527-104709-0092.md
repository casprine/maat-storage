## Description

Add maat catalog list and maat catalog show commands for humans. List should support apps, patterns, decisions, and opportunities with compact scannable columns. Show should render a structured detail view for one object.

## Acceptance

- maat catalog list apps shows app name, summary, language, license, category, and review date when present.
- maat catalog list patterns shows title, category, observed apps, and related Maat work.
- maat catalog show <id-or-slug> renders summary, metadata, links, notes, related patterns, and related goals or tickets.
